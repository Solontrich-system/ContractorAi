What is ContractorAI Suite?

ContractorAI Suite is a smart finance toolbox designed from the ground up for people who work on-site and on the move. It replaces the chaos of manual bookkeeping with an intelligent, mobile-first system that handles invoicing, payments, ledger tracking, bank transfers, and receipt capture — all without needing an accountant or a desk.

Who uses it?

Independent Contractors & Freelancers — manage client invoices, log income, and track every job from quote to payment.
Construction Companies & Crews — monitor project spend, capture receipts on-site, and reconcile supplier EFTs without leaving the job.
Small Business Owners — get a clear picture of cash flow, outstanding invoices, and monthly expenses from a single dashboard.

What it does for your business

Get paid faster with professional invoices sent directly from your phone
Never lose a receipt — scan and organise on the spot
Know exactly where your money is at any point in time
Track subscriptions and EFTs so nothing slips through unnoticed
Reduce admin time so you can focus on the work, not the paperwork
Go into tax season with clean, organised records already in place

Features

Invoices Draft professional invoices in seconds, send them directly to clients, track when they've been viewed, log payments, and chase outstanding amounts — all from one screen. No more chasing payments over text.
Ledger A live running ledger of every transaction — income, expenses, and transfers. Filter by date, category, or client. See your profit and loss at a glance without needing accounting software or a bookkeeper.
EFTs Log every bank transfer, supplier payment, and subscription. Flag recurring EFTs so you always know what's being deducted and when. Instantly spot unexpected charges before they become a problem.
Receipts Point your camera at any paper or digital receipt and ContractorAI Suite captures, reads, and files it automatically. No more crumpled receipts in your work boots — everything is stored, searchable, and ready for tax time.

APK Install Guide

How to Install on Android

Follow these steps carefully — the whole process takes under 2 minutes.

  1. 1
    Tap the download button above.
    Your browser will begin downloading app-release.apk. If Chrome asks "This type of file can harm your device", tap Download anyway — this warning appears for all APKs, not just this one.
  2. 2
    Enable "Install unknown apps" for your browser.
    Android blocks APKs from outside the Play Store by default. Go to Settings → Apps, find your browser (e.g. Chrome), tap it, then select Install unknown apps and toggle Allow from this source to ON. You only need to do this once.
  3. 3
    Open the downloaded file.
    Pull down your notification shade and tap the completed download — app-release.apk — or open your Files / Downloads app and locate it there. Tap the file to launch the Android installer.
  4. 4
    Review permissions and tap Install.
    Android will show you a summary of what the app can access. Review these, then tap Install. The installation only takes a few seconds.
  5. 5
    Handle Play Protect warnings (if any).
    If you see "Play Protect blocked this app", tap More details → Install anyway. This is a standard warning for apps distributed outside the Play Store and does not mean the app is unsafe.
  6. 6
    Open ContractorAI Suite.
    Once installed, tap Open on the installer screen, or find ContractorAI Suite in your app drawer. Sign in or create an account to get started.
  7. 7
    Updating the app in future.
    When a new version is released, download the latest app-release.apk from this page and install it — Android will automatically update over the existing installation without losing any of your data.
